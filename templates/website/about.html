About WICI Uganda

A professional chapter advancing women leadership and participation in Uganda's construction and infrastructure ecosystem.

Our Mission

To empower and connect women in construction and infrastructure through professional development, advocacy, and strategic partnerships.

Our Vision

An inclusive, high-performing construction and infrastructure sector where women are visible, influential, and fully represented.

Professional Excellence

We foster standards, competence, and integrity.

Strategic Influence

We engage policymakers and industry leaders on inclusive growth.

Community Impact

We build pathways for women and young professionals to thrive.
